Why CrowdCare

People in smaller cities often struggle to find help in time, while willing volunteers have no single place to discover who needs them.

Our mission

Make it as easy to find help for your neighbourhood as it is to order food. We connect volunteers, NGOs, and local citizens on one simple website.

Goal: 10,000 volunteers · 50,000 families helped

The problem

Requests for help are scattered across WhatsApp, posters, and calls. Many never reach the right people in time, even though volunteers live nearby.

One hub can turn good intentions into real support
